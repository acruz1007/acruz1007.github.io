Terry Foxs' Marathon Run

This picture is meaningful to me because Terry Fox was a Canadian who ran for Cancer for 143 days straight with a prosthetic leg until cancer forced him to stop. He averaged a marathon per day, running a total of 3,339 miles. As a fellow Canadian, this is a historical event that I take pride in.
